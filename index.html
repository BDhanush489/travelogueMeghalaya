About

Meghalaya, a state in northeastern India, offers a fantastic blend of natural beauty and friendly people. It's known as "The abode of clouds" and is a haven for nature lovers. The people are very friendly. Meghalaya is the best place for solo trips.


Natural Bounty: Meghalaya is home to various fruits, veggies, spices, and medicinal plants. This means delicious food and natural remedies.

Safety: Meghalaya is one of the safest states to visit, especially for women and solo travelers. The locals are very friendly and welcoming.

Unique Textiles: Skilled artisans in Meghalaya create beautiful woven textiles that showcase the state's identity.

Unique Features: Meghalaya is famous for its heavy monsoons, rich traditional festivals, and dramatic landscapes, including rolling hills and waterfalls.

Places

Shillong: The capital city of Meghalaya, often called the "Scotland of the East," offers a blend of urban charm and natural beauty. Don't miss Ward's Lake, Shillong Peak, and the Don Bosco Museum.

Cherrapunji: Famous for its lush landscapes and record-breaking rainfall, Cherrapunji boasts attractions like Nohkalikai Falls, Double Decker Living Root Bridge, and Mawsmai Caves.

Jaintia Hills: Explore the stunning Krang Suri Falls and the ancient Nartiang Monoliths, which provide a glimpse into the rich history and culture of the Jaintia tribe.

Tura: Located in the Garo Hills, Tura is a peaceful town with attractions like Tura Peak, Siju Caves, and the Balpakram National Park, home to diverse flora and fauna.


Activities

Trekking and Hiking: Meghalaya is a trekker's paradise. You can explore various trekking routes that lead to stunning locations like the Double Decker Living Root Bridge, Nohkalikai Falls, and David Scott Trail. The lush green landscapes and challenging terrain make trekking here an unforgettable experience.

Caving: Meghalaya is famous for its limestone caves. Mawsmai Cave and Siju Cave are popular destinations for spelunkers. Explore these mysterious underground worlds and marvel at the unique cave formations.

River Rafting and Boating: Enjoy the thrill of river rafting on the Umngot River in Dawki. The crystal-clear waters and scenic surroundings make it a memorable adventure. You can also go boating in serene locations like Ward's Lake in Shillong.

Relaxation and Meditation: Meghalaya's serene and picturesque settings make it an ideal place for relaxation and meditation. Find tranquility amidst the hills, lush forests, and near waterfalls.
